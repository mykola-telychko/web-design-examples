Antiquity

(8th century BC - 5th century AD)

The period of flourishing of ancient Greek and Roman cultures, characterized by the development of philosophy, science, art, and literature.

Middle Ages

(5th - 15th centuries)

The period of dominance of the Christian church, development of feudal relations, scholastic philosophy, and Gothic art.

Renaissance

(14th - 16th centuries)

A period of renewed interest in ancient culture, the development of humanism, Renaissance art, and scientific discoveries.

Early Modern Period

(17th - 19th centuries)

A period of development of capitalist relations, the scientific revolution, the Enlightenment, classicism and romanticism in art.

Modern Era

(20th - 21st centuries)

A period of scientific and technological progress, globalization, development of mass culture, postmodernism.
